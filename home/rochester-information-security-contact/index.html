INITIAL CONSULTATION

Every engagement begins with a complimentary consultation. This conversation allows us to:

- Understand your current security posture and compliance requirements
- Discuss your objectives, timeline, and constraints
- Explore potential approaches and solutions
- Determine if there's a good fit for working together
- Answer any questions you have about frameworks, certifications, or processes

There's no obligation and no sales pressure, just an honest discussion about your needs and how we might be able to help.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Response Time: We typically respond to inquiries within one business day.

Consultation Format: Initial consultations are usually 30-45 minutes and can be conducted via phone or video call, whichever you prefer.

After Consultation: If there's a potential fit, we'll provide a detailed proposal outlining the approach, deliverables, timeline, and investment required. You'll have everything you need to make an informed decision.
